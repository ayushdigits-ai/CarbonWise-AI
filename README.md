# 🌱 CarbonWise AI

CarbonWise AI is an AI-powered sustainability assistant that helps users understand, track, and reduce their carbon footprint through personalized insights, smart recommendations, and gamified challenges.

## 🚀 Problem Statement

Design a solution that helps individuals understand, track, and reduce their carbon footprint through simple actions and personalized insights.

## 💡 Solution

CarbonWise AI transforms complex carbon emission data into actionable daily habits. Users can monitor activities, analyze their environmental impact, and receive AI-generated recommendations to reduce emissions.

## ✨ Features

### 📊 Carbon Footprint Calculator
Calculate emissions based on:
- Transportation
- Electricity Usage
- Food Habits
- Shopping Behavior
- Waste Generation

### 🤖 AI Sustainability Coach
Personalized recommendations such as:
- Travel optimization
- Energy-saving suggestions
- Sustainable lifestyle tips

### 📈 Carbon Dashboard
- Daily Tracking
- Weekly Trends
- Monthly Reports
- Progress Visualization

### 🎯 Eco Challenges
Gamified sustainability missions:
- Public Transport Challenges
- Energy Saving Challenges
- Plastic Reduction Challenges

### 🔮 Carbon Impact Simulator
"What if" analysis:
- Switching to EV
- Reducing AC Usage
- Vegetarian Days
- Solar Energy Adoption

### 🏆 Community Leaderboard
Encourages collective environmental impact.
